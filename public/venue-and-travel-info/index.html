Venue and Travel Info

Venue

The conference will take place in a historic building located in the heart of the left bank of the Moldau River. Currently home to the Faculty of Mathematics and Physics, this building once served as a Jesuit monastery. The address is Malostranské nám. 25, 118 00, Praha 1

Accommodation

There are many hotels near the venue; however, we recommend booking soon.

Travel Info

Prague is easily accessible by plane (via the Václav Havel Airport) or by train (the main railway station is only several stops away from the venue by public transport). Uber and other affordable taxi networks such as Bolt operate in Prague and can be recommended rather than picking taxis in the streets.
